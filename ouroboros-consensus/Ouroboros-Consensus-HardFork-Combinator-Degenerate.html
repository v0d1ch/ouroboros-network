newtype DegenFork b Source #

Degenerate hard fork with a single era

NOTE: It is important to realize that in general

HardForkBlock '[b]

and

DegenFork b

may behave differently. Crucially, they might have

  • different serialization formats, where the former uses a serialization format that is forward-compatible with hard fork transitions, whereas the latter may well not be
  • related to the previous point, it will have its own network protocol versioning

The main use of DegenFork is for testing, and as evidence that all type class instances that are required for the hard fork are present.
